Privacy Policy

How DoCon+ collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

Welcome to DoCon+. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how DoCon+ collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our website, mobile application, or any related services.

By accessing or using DoCon+, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

To provide our services effectively, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Company or Organization Name
  • Profile Information
  • Business Information
  • Documents uploaded by users
  • Project details
  • Business records
  • Notes and other information voluntarily submitted

Technical Information

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • App Usage Data
  • Login History
  • Cookies and Analytics Data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide access to our services
  • Store and organize your documents
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Maintain security of our platform
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information. These include:

  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Encrypted communication where applicable
  • Restricted access to user data
  • Regular security monitoring
  • Secure authentication mechanisms

Although we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your information may only be shared:

  • With trusted service providers assisting us in operating our platform
  • When required by applicable law
  • To comply with legal processes or government requests
  • To protect our legal rights and prevent fraud
  • During business mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring

5. Cookies

Our website and application may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve performance
  • Enhance security

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features may not function properly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Fulfill contractual obligations
  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

When information is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Update your account details
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests may be subject to legal and operational limitations.

Our platform may integrate with third-party services for authentication, analytics, communication, or other features.

These services operate under their own privacy policies, and DoCon+ is not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Children's Privacy

DoCon+ is intended for users who are at least 18 years of age or otherwise legally permitted to use our services.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be published on this page with the revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
